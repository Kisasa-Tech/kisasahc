Your questions answered

Empowering growth through opportunity

Explore our strategies for workforce enhancement and youth empowerment.

Which sectors do you support?

We serve major sectors financial services, BPO, call centres,ICT, software development, hospitality, Solar, retail, and related industries. Programmes are tailored to business needs.

How do you tailor training?

We align training with your business objectives and workforce needs, delivering targeted upskilling solutions.

What does learnership management cover?

We manage recruitment, onboarding, funding, payroll, accredited training, compliance, and ongoing support for each learner.

How do business partnerships operate?

We co-create programmes with partners, focusing on measurable impact and inclusive workforce development.

What are the employer advantages?

Employers benefit from skilled talent, improved BBBEE scores, cost saving on salaries and increased productivity through our programmes.

Who is eligible to join?

Business & South African youth seeking skills and experience, and businesses aiming to grow their workforce, can participate. we operate nationally and expanding into Europe and the middle east in the near future
